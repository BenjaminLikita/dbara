What we cover

Three layers.
One posture.

Each engagement starts narrow — find what's exposed, fix it, and build the habits that keep it closed. We don't sell retainers for problems we haven't found yet.

A1

Infrastructure audits

Full-stack review of cloud configuration, network segmentation, and identity controls. You get a ranked list of what's actually exploitable, not a 90-page checklist.

A2

Penetration testing

Manual, scoped attacks against your applications and infrastructure — run by people, not scanners. Every finding ships with a reproduction path and a fix.

A3

Managed monitoring

24/7 detection and response, tuned to your environment over the first 30 days so alerts mean something. Escalation paths your team actually uses.

A4

Compliance readiness

SOC 2, ISO 27001, and NDPR alignment without the theatre. We map controls to what you've already built and close the real gaps first.

A5

Incident response

On-call retainer for the day something goes wrong. Containment, root cause, and a post-mortem your board can actually read.

A6

Team training

Practical security workshops for engineers and operations staff — phishing, secrets handling, and incident drills, built around your actual stack.

How we work

Four phases.
No skipping ahead.

Order matters here — each phase produces the input the next one needs. We won't propose monitoring before we've confirmed what's worth monitoring.

Phase 01 — Map

Discover the real surface

Inventory every system, credential, and integration — including the ones nobody remembers setting up.

Phase 02 — Test

Attack it deliberately

Controlled exploitation of what we found, scoped to avoid disruption to live operations.

Phase 03 — Close

Fix what's exploitable

Prioritized remediation, implemented alongside your team rather than handed off as a report.

Phase 04 — Hold

Keep it closed

Monitoring, alerting, and review cadences calibrated to your environment — and revisited quarterly.
